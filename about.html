Our story begins in 1763... when our trade ship bound for new worlds was hit by the storm

Stranded on the nearby island, we started setting up camps and building port waiting for rescue...

After years of using various ingredients traded from passing ships and local ingredients, we found what we believe cuisine like no other

Along the way, we started our restaurant that served as a kitchen. We used what sea and land provided coming up with weird and uncommon recipes.
